Getting Started

With an attractive and elegant interface, OKCupid steals the show totally! The website is user-friendly and comfy with bold letters and colorful backgrounds. Anybody can sign up and use most of its interesting features for free. If you are new to online dating then OKCupid will be the best place to try your luck.

Sign up

OKCupid uses a simple questionnaire and registration process. You can sign up by mentioning your date of birth, location, zip code and email address or simply use your Facebook account to sign into the website.

Once you sign in, OKCupid prompts you to answer a set of ten simple questions. They are light-hearted and witty ones. These questions do not bog your mind or tense you out. OKCupid definitely knows about first impression.

Create profile

Once you create an OKCupid account and answer the ten questions, the site navigates to a set of random matches. You can select three matches that suit your taste and the site even allows you to skip this step if you want to take things slow.

OKCupid takes you to the profile page after this process. You just have to be cool and fill in information about yourself. Unlike, other dating sites that ask you to fill hundreds of questions, OKCupid keeps it simple and lighter.

The profile page is divided into three parts; “About”, “Photos” and “Questions”. In the ‘about’ section, users must describe about themselves. Some of the questions include I’m really good at, six things I cannot do without, favorite books, music, movies and on a typical Friday I am.

You must also fill in some basic information like who you are looking for, age group, relationship status, location, whether you are here for dating, new friends or casual sex. Basic details section includes information about your height, body type, relationship type, ethnicity, religion, habits, zodiac sign, education, kids and language.

Upload photos

Once you create your profile, OKCupid lets you enhance it by adding a face for your profile. You can upload photos from your computer or any other photo website. But photos are mandatory to increase attention and visibility of your profile. OKCupid is strict with photos and does not allow photos with nudity, extreme close ups, pets and artwork.

Questions

This is the most unique and important feature of OKCupid. The website uses numerical percentage to find compatibility between members. The higher the percentage is the greater are the chances of finding better matches.

Questions play an important role in increasing this numerical percentage. To find better matches, users are prompted to answer as many as questions possible. The basic requirement for finding matches is to answer at least 25 questions.

There are almost 4,000 questions in the system so it’s advisable to enjoy them as you answer. They are multiple choice questions; where you must give your answer along with the answer you are expecting from your partner.

The answer must be rated according to its importance. The default answers are a little, somewhat and very. It also allows you to explain the answer for clarification purposes but it’s totally optional.

Find Your Mate

OKCupid uses the responses of users to bring in their ideal matches. There are two types of match search options; browse matches and quick match.

Using browse matches, members can look for potential partners based on looks, background, availability, personality, vices and questions. You can also browse matches based on location, age group, photos and relationship status. The results will display the percentage they match you.

Quick match brings you a random set of matches that displays their full profile. You can rate or skip the profile. Advanced search options include keywords, joining date, etc.

Quiver is a unique feature that is available only after you complete 25 match questions. It shows three matches at a time and replaces it when it becomes empty. OKCupid assess 55 different personality traits to bring in the best match.

After answering 500 questions, OKCupid allows its users to write their own questions on the “Improve Matches” page.

Interacting With Matches

Members can interact with their matches through messages. If you message one match, you will receive a new one the next day. If you ignore it, then it will be replaced after seven days. The traditional options for communication like emails and messages are available on the website.

OKCupid uses some quirky ways to spark interest between matches. It has created tests such as The "Nerd? Geek? Or Dork?” Or “3 Variable Funny Test" to compare the compatibility between matches. These tests are funny ways to interact with matches because the answers will not affect your numerical percentage.

Bottom Line

OKCupid is a free online dating site that provides a well-designed interface, interactive features, personality assessment tests and a math-compatibility factor to help millions of singles find ever-lasting love in their lives.

Personally, OKCupid seems to be the best out of all dating websites. The colorful and attractive layout just soothes your mind and instantly lifts your mood. The atmosphere on OKCupid is very light and friendly that resembles to a social media platform.

It’s very easy to search for people, connect, interact and become friends with other users. There is no fear of sexual contents and photos on OKCupid which makes the dating experience comfortable and fun. It’s a must try for all teenagers trying to hit their heart with the emotion of love.
