Tips of Writing a User Review

  • Would you try this site again?
  • What is the feature you most satisfied with or most do not like? Please also explain why.
  • Make sure to give a fair and balanced review - it will be better if you describe both the pros and cons!
  • Do not state something as a fact unless you know it to be true. Stating your opinions as facts may mislead people.
  • Do not include personal information about yourself (your address, email address, telephone number) or someone else in the review.
  • Please try to keep posts on topic and use a clear title that describes what your review is about.
  • Do not copy and paste reviews from other sites or enter any copyrighted comments. Also, please don't reference the Editor's Review.
  • Do not use profanity or inflammatory comments.
  • Do not include references to other sites and pricing information (which will go out of date).
  • Review submissions may be edited by staff for spelling, grammar, foul language, etc.
  • HerpesDatingSites.biz is not responsible or liable in any way for ratings and reviews posted by its visitors.
